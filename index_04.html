Welcome to the About-Pingu page!

Things You should know about Pingu:

Pingu is a 5 year old penguin who lives with his family in an igloo at the South Pole. His best friend is a seal named Robby.

His little sister Pinga came out of the egg two years ago. Both Pinga and Pingu love eating pancakes! They also like to take the sled and play tricks on Grandfather who lives a few icebergs away.

This is my family:

Father

Pingu's dad works at the postoffice and also has a really cool snow-mobile to deliver the mail.

Mother

Mother makes the best pancakes in the world and loves her two children very much.

Pingu

Pingu is a little troublemaker with a good heart and lots of ideas that get him into trouble.

Pinga

Pinga is Pingu's little sister. Her favourite activity is to follow Pingu around.
